Our bank offers savings accounts, credit cards, and personal loans.

Savings Accounts:
- Earn 1.5% interest per annum.
- Minimum maintaining balance is $500.
- Withdrawals are limited to 3 per month.

Credit Cards:
- Monthly payments are due every 25th.
- A $35 fee applies for late payments.
- Cashback of 1% on groceries and 2% on fuel.

Personal Loans:
- Available from $5,000 to $50,000.
- Repayment terms range from 1 to 5 years.
- Interest rate is fixed at 6.5% annually.
